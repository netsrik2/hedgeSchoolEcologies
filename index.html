Hedge School Ecologies

Researching local history as a lens through which to explore and connect with the rural environment of Cavan and adjoining counties in ecologically sustainable ways.

Irish hedge school histories provide rich learning models and patterns of interaction with the environment, spaces for learning from and within the hedgerows, opening up new imaginative possibilities for future models of both learning and ecological sustainability from small to large scale.

The Greek root of ecology, oikos, meaning host, is articulated in the dramatization of the scripts, staging scenes for learning through narratives connecting ideas. Hedge School Ecologies becomes a stage set for interacting with the bogland, as a marginal territory.
